Born in France, raised in Korea, the Philippines, and France again before moving to the US, Jin is a true citizen of the world (and an avid traveller). He holds a Bachelor's degree in Architecture from the University of Southern California and worked as a designer at the boutique architecture firm, JFAK, in Los Angeles. Currently, he is pursuing his Master of Urban Planning at GSAPP, Columbia University. He is also working as a project associate at GSAPP's Housing Lab, a space for creative and innovative discussions around housing in New York, led by Dean Amale Andraos. Jin's interests include urban data analytics and learning about successful public-private partnerships fostering greater economic development.
