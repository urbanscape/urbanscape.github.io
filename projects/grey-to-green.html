Grey to Green

The Grey-to-Green Energy Transition studio was tasked with identifying strategies and opportunities related to the eventual closure of peaker power plants in New York City, balancing community priorities with future energy-related demands. This studio was conducted under Columbia Graduate School of Architecture, Planning, and Preservation's Urban Planning program for the Spring 2020 semester. The studio served the New York City Environmental Justice Alliance (NYC-EJA) as its client under the guidance of faculty Athony Borelli and Graham Trelstad, and teaching assistant, Gayatri Kawlra.

Team Members: Justin Barton, Riley Burchell, HK Dunston, Yuning Feng, Yiyi Jiang, Jin Hong Kim, Geon Woo Lee, Hongseog Lee, Thiago Lee, Priska Marianne, Xifan Wang, Angel Yin
